Computer Engineering




The ever-widening field of computers has opened new avenues for competent and trained technicians who could ably to look after production, testing, installation & servicing of micro, mini computers and allied equipment. With the advent of LANS, WANS & INTERNET, the need for maintaining, servicing and repairing of computers, computer based control systems, network systems, network installations & network administration has become most essential. The objective of the course is to gain a good understanding of software, hardware development, the way computers are designed and built for general and specific users, various software and network, maintenance of networks, the mode of achieving the required tasks easily and quickly. The diploma engineers trained in these disciplines will have exciting job opportunities in the expanding computer industry and software development.


Civil Engineering




The Construction Technology technician (Diploma Holder) can take up supervision of construction work of all types, surveying and leveling and data collection. He / She can also be take care of material management. He / She can get employment as an overseer/ Junior Engineer in infrastructure development projects, Municipal Corporations, Building and Communication Department, Irrigation and Power Department, State Electricity Board, Housing Board, Building Repairs and Reconstruction, Public works departments, CPWD, national highway authority, military engineering services, railway engineering, private construction firms, etc. He/ She can also find employment in consulting and architectural firms or work independently as a contractor. Also he/ she can start a small scale industry related to construction like manufacturing of Paver Blocks, tiles, manhole covers, furrow cement concrete products, fabrication, road dividers, bricks making, mortar blocks, stone crushers etc.


Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering




The emphasis of this course is on testing, modification and development of circuits in control of power to industrial drives and control of signals in test process instrumentation and communication. The job prospects of a diploma holder in Industrial Electronics are as Technical Assistant, Supervisor in quality control, Production Development and Maintenance department of industries which Manufacture and use electronics equipment. There is much scope for self employment in manufacturing of electronic equipment and gadgets including Radio and T.V. Receivers and VCR.


Mechanical Engineering




The Mechanical engineering branch emphasis on manufacturing in Engineering Industries. The diploma holder can work in Engineering Department, Inspection, Quality Control, Maintenance and Production shops and Industrial Engineering Department in industries involved in manufacturing of industrial machine tools, automobiles, ancillary industries, electrical machinery etc. and the shop floor management in the Production Industries. Technician in these branches will have good scope in Research & Development Department.

About Us

Vidyavardhini means a Body committed to enhancement of Knowledge. Vidyavardhini was established as a registered society in 1970 by late Padmashri H. G. alias Bhausaheb Vartak for the noble cause of education in rural areas. Vidyavardhini College of Engineering and Technology, Vasai is located on the sprawling campus of Vidyavardhini, spread over an area of 12.27 acres. It is a short, two minutes walk from Vasai Road (W) Railway Station. The college is also accessible by road from Mumbai. Vidyavardhini Society received approval from AICTE to start the new college of Engineering & Technology with effect from July, 1994. The college is affiliated to the University of Mumbai for the four year degree program leading to the degree of Bachelor of Engineering.
